\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Object representing a 3D philosopher in the Dining Philosophers\textquotesingle{} problem. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{class_philosopher3_d}{Philosopher3D}} class contains variables and methods necessary for representing a philosopher at a table. Each \mbox{\hyperlink{class_philosopher3_d}{Philosopher3D}} may acquire or release the fork to his left or to his right (or both), with his state changing accordingly.
