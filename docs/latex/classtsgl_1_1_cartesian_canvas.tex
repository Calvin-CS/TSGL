\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classtsgl_1_1_canvas}{Canvas}} extended with Cartesian drawing operations. 

\mbox{\hyperlink{classtsgl_1_1_cartesian_canvas}{Cartesian\+Canvas}} provides a \mbox{\hyperlink{classtsgl_1_1_canvas}{Canvas}} with a Cartesian coordinate system for ease of plotting. \begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
While on a regular \mbox{\hyperlink{classtsgl_1_1_canvas}{Canvas}}, pixels higher on the screen have a lower y-\/value, {\bfseries{on a \mbox{\hyperlink{classtsgl_1_1_cartesian_canvas}{Cartesian\+Canvas}}, pixels higher on the screen have a higher y-\/value.}} 
\end{DoxyNote}
