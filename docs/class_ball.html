Detailed Description

We can't play Pong without a Ball!

class Ball

Creates the ball needed in order to play Pong.

The constructor determines the speed and direction of the ball.

The draw() method draws the ball onto the Canvas object.

The ball's position is always reset after a point has been earned in the game.

Collisions with the paddle or the boundaries are not handled here; they are handled in the Pong class' draw() method.

See also
Paddle class, Pong class.
